Vegan Lasagna

Return to index

Slice of lasagna on a plate

A delicous plant-based lasagna recipe using all vegan ingredients. It is a little time consuming to make, but its worth the effort. Takes about 2 hours to cook and 30 minutes to prepare. Yields 8 servings.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 1/2 cups chopped onion
  • 5 tbsp minced garlic
  • 4 (14.5oz) cans stewed tomatoes
  • 1/3 cup tomato paste
  • 3/4 cup chopped fresh basil
  • 1/2 cup chopped parsley
  • 1 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper plus extra to taste
  • 1 (16oz) package lasagna noodles
  • 2lbs firm tofu
  • 3 (10oz) packages of frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained

Steps

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large heavy saucepan over medium heat. Add the onions and saute for about 5 mins. Add the garlic and cook 5 mins. more
  2. Place tomatoes, tomato paste, basil and parsley in the saucepan. Stir well, turn heat to low and let simmer for one hour. Add salt and pepper.
  3. While the sauce is cooking prepare the noodles as instructed on the box.
  4. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F
  5. Place the tofu in a large bowl. Add the remaining garlic, basil, and parsley. Add salt and pepper, and mash well.
  6. Assemble the lasagna: Spread 1 cup of sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch casserole dish. Arrange a single layer of noodles and sprinkle one third of the tofu mixture over the noodles. Distribute half of the spinach over the tofu. Ladle 1 1/12 cups of sauce over the tofu, and top with another layer of noodles. Sprinkle with 1/3 of tofu mixture, remaining spinach, and sauce. Place a final layer noodles over the top. Spread the remaining tofu and tomato sauce over.
  7. Cover with foil and bake the lasagna for 30 minutes.
