Air Fried Veggie sticks

A yummy alternative to French Fries. Toss root vegetables with your choice of seasoning, then air fry and serve with citrus-herb dip.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb assorted root vegetables (carrots, parsnips, beets, onions, sweet potatoes) peeled and cut into bite size pieces
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp of your favorite seasong blend
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/2 cup plain vegan yogurt
  • 1 tsp lemon zest
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh herbs
  • 1/8 tsp salt
  • 1/8 ground pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the air fyer to 400 degrees F.
  2. Add prepared root vegtables to a large bowl and toss with oil, 1/4 tsp salt, and 1/4 tsp pepper.
  3. Air-fry, turning halfway through, until the sticks are tender and golden, about 18 minutes.
  4. Prepare the citrus herb dip by combning the remaining ingredients.
