Chocolate Chip Cookies

Who doesn't love warm chocolate chip cookies out of the oven? This easy to make recipe will be ready to eat in about 25 minutes. Makes approximately 36 cookies.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup coconut oil
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 1/2 cup almond milk
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup vegan chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Cream coconut oil and brown sugar together in a bowl with and electric mixer, until thoroughly combined. Mix in almond milk and vanilla until incorporated.
  3. Whisk in flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in a separate bowl. Add the flour mixture to the almond milk mixture, stirring until combined. The dough will be crumbly. Fold in the chocolate chips.
  4. Roll the dough into tablespoon-sized balls and place on an ungreased baking sheet. Flatten each ball.
  5. Bake in preheated oven for 8 minutes or until edges are golden brown.
